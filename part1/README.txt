KEYBOARD & MOUSE CONTROLS
-------------------------
- Orb (sphere) movement:
    W / S       move orb forward / backward
    A / D       move orb left / right
    Q / E       move orb up / down

- Camera:
    Left mouse drag     rotate (orbit) around the scene
    Right mouse drag    pan
    Mouse wheel         zoom in / out
